Enumeration Type Documentation

enum engine::CLOSURE_TYPE
Enumerator
NO_CLOSURE 

Describes regular field, where objects can not move outside of bounds.

HORIZONTAL_CLOSURE 

If an object goes beyond the boundaries of the field while moving 'x' coordinate it'll be places at the opposite side.

VERTICAL_CLOSURE 

If an object goes beyond the boundaries of the field while moving 'y' coordinate it'll be places at the opposite side.

FULL_CLOSURE 

It is combination of all closure types.
